# Community Code of Conduct

We want project spaces to be safe, welcoming, and useful.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, patient, and specific.
- Critique ideas and code without attacking people.
- Respect privacy, identity, background, experience level, and accessibility needs.
- Accept constructive feedback and correct mistakes.
- Keep discussions relevant to the project.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized attention, doxxing, deliberate
misinformation, repeated disruption, and publishing another person’s private information
are not allowed.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately through the contact method in
[SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md); do not open a public issue containing sensitive details.
Maintainers may edit or remove content, issue a warning, temporarily restrict
participation, or permanently ban participation when needed to protect the community.
Reports will be handled as confidentially and fairly as practical.

This policy applies in repository discussions, issues, pull requests, reviews, and other
spaces where someone represents this project.
